Lifestyle & Prevention

Nutrition & Diet

    {% if risk == 2 %}
  • Strict Keto/Paleo: Minimize all simple carbs.
  • Zero Added Sugar: Completely eliminate processed sweeteners.
  • High Fiber: At least 35g of fiber daily from vegetables.
    • {% elif risk == 1 %}
  • Low Carb: Limit grains and bread to one serving/day.
  • Natural Sugars: Switch from soda to whole fruits.
  • Healthy Fats: Use olive oil instead of butter.
    • {% else %}
  • Balanced Diet: Follow a standard Mediterranean diet.
  • Mindful Eating: Avoid overeating and late-night snacks.
  • Hydration: Drink 2L of water daily.
    • {% endif %}

Physical Activity

    {% if risk == 2 %}
  • Daily Aerobic: 45-60 mins of brisk walking daily.
  • Supervised Training: Recommended for BP management.
    • {% elif risk == 1 %}
  • Moderate Active: 150 mins of cycling/walking weekly.
  • Strength: 2 days of bodyweight exercises.
    • {% else %}
  • Maintain: Continue regular gym/sports activities.
  • 10k Steps: Aim for consistent daily step counts.
    • {% endif %}

Monitoring

    {% if risk == 2 %}
  • Bi-Weekly Checks: HbA1c and Lipid tests every 3 months.
  • Daily BP Logs: Monitor and record mornings/evenings.
    • {% elif risk == 1 %}
  • Bi-Annual Checks: Standard blood work every 6 months.
  • Weekly BP: Check at least once a week.
    • {% else %}
  • Annual Physical: Full checkup once a year.
    • {% endif %}

Stress & Sleep

    {% if risk == 2 %}
  • Deep Sleep: Use sleep tracking; aim for 8+ hours.
  • Coping Mechanisms: Professional therapy or daily meditation.
    • {% elif risk == 1 %}
  • Digital Detox: No screens 1 hour before bed.
  • Relaxation: Evening walks or reading to de-stress.
    • {% else %}
  • Consistency: Maintain regular sleep/wake cycles.
    • {% endif %}
